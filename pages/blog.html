About Crazy Cars

The business is born from a long heritage of dealing in sports cars, stemming from the owners personal ownership of numerous contemporary Lamborghini models over 40 years ago.

This passion for collecting sports cars inevitably led to more active buying and selling of the cars, which in turn led to the establishment of sports cars as a separate trading entity.

The success of the business over many years trading, has led to its position as a globally recognised centre of excellence for sports car sales, with thousands of satisfied customers all over the world.
